Brand & Press Kit

What is Socialmesh?

Socialmesh is a mobile app for communicating over Meshtastic mesh radio networks. It enables off-grid messaging, location sharing, and real-time updates without cellular service.

Press Kit Checklist

  1. Brand name — Socialmesh (one word, capital S)
  2. Product tagline — Off-Grid Communication Companion
  3. Logo — Use the app icon on dark or light backgrounds
  4. Screenshots — Show messages, map, and dashboard widgets

Product Facts

Category Details
Platforms iOS and Android

Key Features Messaging, maps, presence, automations, widgets

Marketing FAQ

Q: Can I use Socialmesh logos and screenshots?

Yes — you can use official Socialmesh logos and app screenshots to reference the product in reviews, listings, and editorial content. Please do not alter logos or remove attribution.

Q: How should the name be written?

Use “Socialmesh” as a single word. Avoid “Social Mesh” or “SocialMesh”.

Q: What’s the best way to describe the app?
  • “A Meshtastic client for off-grid messaging”
  • “A modern mesh radio companion for iOS and Android”
  • “Reliable communication beyond cellular coverage”
