Core Features

Everything You Need, Nothing You Don't

Every feature designed to enhance your mesh network experience. From intuitive node management to powerful visualization tools.

Node Management

Star favorites, filter by status, search instantly. Keep track of every node in your mesh with real-time status updates.

Intelligent Messaging

Quick responses, message reactions, read receipts, and rich media support. Communication that feels natural.

Advanced Mapping

See every node in your mesh on a beautiful map. Filter by distance, signal strength, or online status.

3D Globe View

Visualize your entire mesh network on an interactive 3D globe. Perfect for large-scale deployments.

Channel Wizard

Create encrypted channels in seconds. Set privacy levels, encryption keys, and advanced options with guided setup.

Device Settings

Full control over every device parameter. Configure radio settings, power options, display preferences, and more.

Analytics Dashboard

Track network health, message volume, node activity, and more. Data-driven insights for your mesh network.

Presence System

Set your status, see who's online, and know when nodes go active or inactive. Real-time presence awareness.

Firmware Updates

Update device firmware directly from the app. One-tap updates keep your devices running the latest software.

Unlock Your Mesh Network's Full Potential

Emergency responders, Search & Rescue teams, event coordinators, and off-grid enthusiasts choose these tools because they solve real problems and deliver measurable value in the field.

Premium Pack

Theme Pack

Personalize your experience with 12 carefully designed accent colors that flow through every screen. Beyond aesthetics, themes provide instant visual context—use Ocean Blue for maritime operations, Forest Green for hiking trips, or Emergency Red when coordinating response efforts. Each theme transforms buttons, highlights, charts, and indicators throughout the entire app.

  • 12 handcrafted accent colors with full app integration
  • Instant switching—no restart required
  • Visual context for different operational modes

Operator Insight

"Cyan for daily hiking, Magenta for emergency coordination. One glance at my screen tells me which mode I'm operating in—critical when seconds matter."

Premium Pack

Ringtone Library

Audio differentiation is essential when your phone is in your pocket or you're wearing gloves. With 7,000+ RTTTL ringtones—from iconic TV themes to video game classics—you can assign unique sounds to each channel, node, or message type. Know instantly whether that notification is a casual chat, an urgent alert, or a specific team member without looking at your screen.

  • 7,000+ ringtones searchable by title, artist, or category
  • Per-channel and per-node audio assignments
  • Preview before applying—sends directly to device

Operator Insight

"Emergency channel plays a loud siren, family chat gets the Mario coin sound. Even with my phone in my pack, I know exactly what needs attention."

Premium Pack

Widget Dashboard

Build mission-specific dashboards that display exactly what your operation needs. A SAR team leader monitors all member positions and battery levels on one screen. An event coordinator tracks channel activity across multiple sectors. A repeater operator watches signal quality metrics in real-time. The drag-and-drop builder lets you create, save, and switch between dashboard configurations for different scenarios.

  • 9 widget types: maps, graphs, lists, status panels, and more
  • Visual drag-and-drop layout builder
  • Save multiple configurations for different operations

Operator Insight

"Our SAR incident commander tablet shows all team positions, battery levels, and last-heard times in one view. No more switching between screens during active searches."

Premium Pack

Automation Engine

Set it and forget it. Automations run in the background, responding to network events automatically so you can focus on what matters. Configure rules that trigger when battery drops below 20%, when a node goes offline, when you enter or leave a geofenced area, or at scheduled times. Actions include sending messages, broadcasting alerts, changing channels, and playing sounds. Chain multiple conditions with AND/OR logic for sophisticated workflows.

  • Triggers: battery level, time, location, messages, node status
  • Actions: send messages, broadcast alerts, play sounds
  • Compound conditions with AND/OR logic

Operator Insight

"When my solar repeater's battery drops below 20%, it automatically broadcasts a warning to the team and switches to low-power mode. Saved our comms during a 3-day event."

Premium Pack

IFTTT & Webhook Integration

Bridge the gap between your offline mesh network and the connected world. IFTTT integration lets you trigger external services when mesh events occur—log location data to Google Sheets, send SMS through Twilio, flash smart home lights when messages arrive, or post to Slack channels. Works bi-directionally: external events can also trigger mesh broadcasts. Custom webhook support for enterprise systems and home automation platforms.

  • Native IFTTT integration with pre-built templates
  • Custom webhook support for any HTTP endpoint
  • Bi-directional: mesh → internet AND internet → mesh

Operator Insight

"SOS broadcasts from the mesh automatically trigger my home alarm, send SMS to emergency contacts, and log GPS coordinates to a shared spreadsheet. Peace of mind for my family."
