Core Features

Everything You Need, Nothing You Don't

Every feature designed to enhance your mesh network experience. From intuitive node management to powerful visualization tools.

Node Management

Star favorites, filter by status, search instantly. Keep track of every node in your mesh with real-time status updates.

Intelligent Messaging

Quick responses, message reactions, read receipts, and rich media support. Communication that feels natural.

Advanced Mapping

See every node in your mesh on a beautiful map. Filter by distance, signal strength, or online status.

3D Globe View

Visualize your entire mesh network on an interactive 3D globe. Perfect for large-scale deployments.

Channel Wizard

Create encrypted channels in seconds. Set privacy levels, encryption keys, and advanced options with guided setup.

Device Settings

Full control over every device parameter. Configure radio settings, power options, display preferences, and more.

Analytics Dashboard

Track network health, message volume, node activity, and more. Data-driven insights for your mesh network.

Presence System

Set your status, see who's online, and know when nodes go active or inactive. Real-time presence awareness.

Firmware Updates

Update device firmware directly from the app. One-tap updates keep your devices running the latest software.

Premium Features

Go Beyond Basic

Transform your mesh network into something extraordinary. Premium features unlock powerful capabilities that serious operators, emergency responders, and power users rely on daily. Each pack is designed to solve real problems and deliver genuine value.

Premium Pack

Theme Pack

Express yourself with 12 stunning accent colors that transform the entire app experience. From bold magenta to professional navy, find the perfect look that matches your style or helps you quickly identify different devices.

  • 12 handcrafted accent colors with full app integration
  • Instantly switch themes without restarting
  • Color-code different profiles or use cases

Real-World Use

Premium Pack

Ringtone Pack

Never miss a critical message again. Access over 5,000 ringtones to create unique audio identities for different channels, nodes, or message types. From classic alerts to modern tones, find the perfect sound for every situation.

  • 5,000+ professional ringtones organized by category
  • Assign unique tones per channel or contact
  • Preview before selecting, instant application

Real-World Use

"Emergency channel gets a loud alarm, family chat gets a gentle chime. I always know what needs immediate attention."

Premium Pack

Widget Pack

Build custom dashboards that display exactly what matters to you. Combine metrics, visualizations, and controls into personalized interfaces. Perfect for monitoring stations, field operations, or creating specialized views for different team members.

  • Drag-and-drop dashboard builder
  • 20+ widget types: graphs, maps, lists, gauges
  • Multiple layouts for phone, tablet, or external display

Real-World Use

"Our SAR team has a tablet dashboard showing all member positions, battery levels, and recent comms—all on one screen."

Premium Pack

Automation Pack

Set up intelligent rules that respond automatically to network events. From battery warnings to emergency broadcasts, automations ensure critical actions happen even when you can't manually respond. Build complex workflows with simple trigger-action logic.

  • Triggers: battery, time, location, messages, node status
  • Actions: send messages, change channels, play alerts
  • Chain conditions with AND/OR logic

Real-World Use

"When my repeater's battery drops below 20%, it automatically broadcasts a warning and switches to low-power mode."

Premium Pack

IFTTT Integration

Connect your mesh network to the wider world. Trigger webhooks, integrate with smart home systems, log data to spreadsheets, or send alerts through external services. Your mesh becomes part of a larger automation ecosystem.

  • Webhook support for any HTTP endpoint
  • Native IFTTT integration with templates
  • Bi-directional: trigger from mesh OR trigger mesh

Real-World Use

"Mesh SOS messages automatically trigger my home alarm, send SMS to emergency contacts, and log GPS to Google Sheets."
