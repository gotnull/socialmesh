Social & Presence

Know Who's Active Without Guesswork

Socialmesh shows presence the way a LoRa mesh actually works. No fake "online" pings, no instant offline. Everything is inferred from real radio activity and last-heard time.

radar Social & Presence

Presence is the product

Socialmesh is built around presence: who’s active, who’s nearby, and what the mesh is doing right now. It’s not a chat app bolted onto LoRa — it’s a living map of activity that updates only when real packets arrive.

Broadcast Signals, discover nearby nodes, and watch activity decay naturally when radios go quiet. No fake pings, no “online” lies — just the mesh as it is.

Active — heard recently
Fading — silence, still plausible
Inactive — likely offline
Unknown — no recent data

Pro Tip

Use Signals like beacons for ops, meetups, or trailheads. TTL decay keeps the map honest— stale posts disappear without manual cleanup.
